when ramen🍜first appear in japan ?

Ramen appeared in Japan in 1910 when some Chinese cooks in Tokyo made their noodles with kansui, a sodium-carbonate mineral water. Ramen is a Japanese noodle dish that has its roots in Chinese noodle dishes. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a broth; common flavors are soy sauce and miso, with typical toppings including sliced pork, nori (dried seaweed), menma (bamboo shoots), and scallions. Ramen has its origin traced back to Yokohama Chinatown in the early 20th century.
